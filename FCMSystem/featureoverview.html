Step By Step Guide To Set Up your Business On FCMS

Step 1:

Login

1. After getting the credentials through email visit FCMS or login through the offical website at your to right leftlogin page.
2. Credentials are your Mobile No. and Password provided in the email.

Staff shall also use the same portal for the login into their panel.

Step 2:

Dashboard

1. After login successfully you will see the statistics, like Total Project, Total Images, Total Videos and Total Staff.

Staff dashboard will only show the statistics of the project assigned to them only.

Step 3:

Staff Management

1. If you are having a team you can add them using Add Staff.
2. After creation you will have View, Edit & Block operations for the staff account.

You (Business Admin) have to share the credentials with your staff and once created they cannot be deleted.

Step 4:

Project Management

1. Befor adding any content you need to created a project and add the content into it. Project creation is important because after creating the project you can assign it to the staff. To create the project you can click on Add Project.
2. After creation you will have View, Edit & Delete operations for the project.

Staff cannot create the project they can only see the project assigned to them. After deleting a project all the content in the project will also get deleted.

Step 5:

Messages

1. Messages are text message that can be used for text campaigns. So after storing it, you can use it multiple times and with easy inline search functionalities you can find it easily. To add a new message click on Add Message.
2. After creation you will have View, Edit & Delete operations for the message.

Staff can only add, view and edit text messages but cannot delete them. They can only see messages that are in their assigned project list.

Step 6:

Images

1. Images can be used for banner & post campaigns. So after storing it, you can use it multiple times and with easy title search functionalities you can find it easily. To add a new image click on Add Image.
2. After creation you will have View, Edit & Delete operations for the image.

Staff can only add, view and edit images but cannot delete them. They can only see images that are in their assigned project list.

Step 7:

Videos

1. Videos can be used for reel & short video campaigns. So after storing it, you can use it multiple times and with easy title search functionalities you can find it easily. To add a new video click on Add Video.
2. After creation you will have View, Edit & Delete operations for the video.

Staff can only add, view and edit videos but cannot delete them. They can only see videos that are in their assigned project list.

Step 8:

Hashtags

1. Hashtags can be used for reel captions. So after storing it, you can use it multiple times and with easy title search and inline search functionalities you can find it easily. To add a new hashtags click on Add Hashtags.
2. After creation you will have View, Edit & Delete operations for the hashtag.

Staff can only add, view and edit hashtags but cannot delete them. They can only see hashtags that are in their assigned project list.

Step 9:

Bug Reports

1. Bug report helps you (Business Admin) to submit any File & Content Management System (FCMS) related bug to the Super Admin. To submit a bug go to Bug Reports in the navigation paenl.

Staff cannot report any bug only you (Business Admin) can send the bug reports to the Super Admin.

Step 10:

Staff Reports

1. Staff reports help you (Business Admin) to get a daily report about what your Digital Marketing Team has done on regular basis and helps you understand the performance of each staff member. To view staff report go to Staff Reports in the navigation paenl.
2. After creation you will have View & Delete operations for the staff reports.

Staff can report their task only to you (Business Admin). No super admin will be involved in between the staff and the business admin.
